Single-player version of JustSing. Most of the social parts were removed but core playing still there! Plus you can have a leaderboard to compete with global players. It was designed for playing on Set-Top Boxes, while also compatible on all android/iOS devices.

This version was completely implemented by Unity3D, in a 3 people small group. I was in charge of writing scripts for most of the game logics, scenes and user interfaces, where I used some relevant Assetbundles to achieve better outcomes, such as NGUI and CameraPath.

In addition, I had worked on handling 3D models and animations in this game.
