JustSing
Design & Development · Jan 2014 - Feb 2016
JustSing is a strong networking mobile game. The center part in this game is to sing different songs. We have a good audio analysis system inside and will evaluate the singing based on pitches, accuracies, and other factors. The system will give you a score and you can compete with others in a round of game. What’s more, we add extensive social elements to make the game more motivative for players. We had a big virtual community where you can meet people, talk to them, buy virtual items and finish tasks. This fact is actually, in my opinion, related to the field of gamification. A lot of our design work is to investigate how to appeal players and make them sticky within the game. We have to know their behaviors and preferences.

For example, we want players to build social relationships in game. They can have organizations, get into groups or even get married in game. We also have a few game modes to choose. They can compete with people or complete challenges by themselves.

It uses Ogre as rendering engine and CEGUI as basic GUI tool.
You can find the status quo of the game via official website or download it at AppStore.
